About Me

I met Jesus at 11, however, since my parents did not follow Jesus, my life began to look very much like the world around me. By young adulthood I was involved in many illicit, and some illegal activities. At 22, there was an incident that changed my life forever, and drew me back to Jesus. Thus began the adventure of discipleship some 30+ years ago! I have been in Women's ministry for 2 decades and found a sweet spot in ministry speaking at events, encouraging others to join me on this great adventure of loving God with passion and authenticity.

Speaking Topics

Released To Run

Based on Heb 12:1-2, Released to Run is designed to help women find their unique callings in the body of Christ and run the race marked out for them.

Finding Fearlessness

Based on Phil. 4:6-8, Finding Fearlessness is designed to show women practical ways to combat the disease of this age: anxiety.

Jars of Clay

Based on II Cor. 4:7-9, we take a journey with Paul to find peace in our imperfections.

Intimacy with God

Based on Mark 12:30-31, we will take the teaching of Jesus and explore what it looks like for the everyday women to have a love affair with God that is an ecstatic sort of relationship that makes the most rapturous relationship between a man and a women seem like milk and water. CS Lewis

Each topic can be broken into different talks to accommodate overnight retreats or day-long conferences. I also give a devotional made for each topic that I provide to the women at the end of the event.

I am also happy to take a verse, topic or theme of your choice and work with the Holy Spirit to create an original retreat/conference.
